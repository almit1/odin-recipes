Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  1. 1 pizza dough
  2. 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  3. 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  4. 1/4 cup sliced pepperoni
  5. 1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
  6. 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers
  7. 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  8. 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  9. 1 teaspoon dried basil
  10. 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
