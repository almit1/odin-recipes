Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  1. 1 pound ground beef
  2. 1 onion, chopped
  3. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  4. 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  5. 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
  6. 2 tablespoons sugar
  7. 1 teaspoon dried basil leaves
  8. 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  9. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  10. 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  11. 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  12. 1 1/2 cups ricotta cheese
  13. 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  14. 12 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained
